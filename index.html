I am a Post doctoral fellow at the Raman Research Institute.

I did my PhD from Chennai Mathematical Institute (CMI). My research under the supervision of Prof. Govind S. Krishnaswami has been focused on classical and quantum integrable systems and field theories and related topics in theoretical physics.

My doctoral thesis concerns the dynamics and integrability of a scalar field theory dual to the 1+1 dimensional SU(2) Principal chiral model and its reduction to a novel mechanical system describing a class of nonlinear screw-type waves.

My primary interest is in integrable systems and field theories, but I am open to exploring problems in other areas of theoretical and mathematical physics.
